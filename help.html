Frequently Asked Questions



Velox is a company that provides reliable transport that is intended to get our customers and their loved ones to their desired destinations anywhere in South Africa.


To make a booking, click the "BOOK NOW" button which is present on the top right corner of the page. You will be presented with a form which will capture all the necessary details required for us to process your booking. Once we have captured these details, an email containing a summary and state of your trip will be sent to you.


Unfortunately, however rarely, a booking might be unsuccessful because of a number of unforeseen reasons, for example, when all of our drivers are employed and no driver would be available at the time of your desired trip.


However unlikely, we will advise you on days whereby drivers are not fully employed and kindly suggest that you move your booking to one of those days.


To cancel a booking, kindly send an email to the administrator containing the reference number given in the summary email sent to you, and simply expressing your wish to cancel the booking.
