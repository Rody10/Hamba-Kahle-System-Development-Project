About Us

Who Are We?


Velox was founded in 2020 to provide reliable transport that is intended to get our customers and their loved ones to their desired destinations anywhere in South Africa. We strive to provide a safe and convenient transportation system that will allow our customers to plan the perfect trip through the click of a few buttons. As trip bookings can be made for a number of people and vehicles, at Velox, our customers have the utmost freedom to explore the beautiful landscapes of South Africa with us, check their bucket lists and most importantly, make everlasting memories.
